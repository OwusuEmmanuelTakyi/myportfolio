## The problem

Most Ghanaians can't afford a lawyer for everyday disputes — a landlord withholding a deposit, an employer skipping a final paycheck, a faulty product a shop refuses to refund. The law exists to protect them, but it's locked behind legal jargon and consultation fees.

## What I built

For my final year project at the University of Ghana, Legon, I built an AI assistant powered by the Anthropic API that answers questions about tenancy, labour, and consumer rights in plain language — grounded in Ghanaian legal context rather than generic advice.

## What I learned

Testing with 20+ students who had never touched a legal document taught me more about prompt design than any tutorial could. The hardest part wasn't the AI — it was translating statute language into something a first-year student could act on immediately.

This project is what convinced me that AI's biggest impact in Ghana won't be flashy demos — it'll be quietly closing access gaps like this one.
